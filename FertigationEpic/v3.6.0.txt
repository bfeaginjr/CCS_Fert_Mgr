Release Notes

## v3.6.0
1. **New Feature**: FIFO (First In First Out)-based queue program. 
   - Programs can be queued in sequence and execute according to the sequence. 
   - Programs can be enqueued and dequeued from the list anytime, with automatic updates to the queue list.
2. **New Feature**: New Mixed I/O card (GRV-MM1001-10) can be added to modules 12 and 13.
   - Inputs and outputs can be assigned to the same card.
   - Channels 0 to 3 on both cards can be used as 4-20mA inputs, and channels 4 to 9 can be used as digital outputs.
3. Added custom zone control script: manages zones 1-5 and irrigation valves 1-9 with timers; activates VFD for pressure/flow; deactivates under specified conditions.
